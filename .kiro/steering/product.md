# Product Overview

IntervuAI Pro is an AI-powered interview platform that automates the technical interview process for candidates.

## Core Functionality

- **Resume Analysis**: Upload candidate resumes (PDF format) and extract key information including name, email, experience, skills, and inferred job position
- **Dynamic Question Generation**: Generate personalized interview questions based on candidate profile and target role (10 technical, 3 soft skills, 2 communication questions)
- **Real-time Interview Session**: Present questions one-by-one with timer tracking and response collection
- **AI-Powered Evaluation**: Evaluate candidate responses across multiple dimensions (technical accuracy, communication clarity, relevance)
- **Comprehensive Assessment**: Generate detailed interview reports with scores, recommendations, strengths, and areas for improvement

## Key Features

- Session-based interview management with unique session IDs
- PDF text extraction for resume processing
- JSON-based AI response parsing with fallback mechanisms
- Progress tracking and navigation through interview questions
- Detailed scoring breakdown per question and overall assessment
- Export capability for interview reports
